Gravity of Closed Strings

My research centers on one question:

What is the gravitational theory naturally implied by
closed string theory?

Research is driven by questions, not by calculations.*

This question motivates my current research program, in which Double Field Theory serves as the working geometric framework.

Research Vision

The question is not whether string theory contains gravity. It does. The question is how string theory organizes gravity. Closed strings contain the universal massless sector {g, B, φ}. General Relativity successfully geometrizes the metric. The central question is therefore: What geometry describes the full closed-string gravitational multiplet?

Working Hypothesis The current working hypothesis is that Double Field Theory provides the natural geometric framework for the universal closed-string massless sector.

Research Themes

Geometry

Doubled-yet-gauged spacetime, coordinate gauge symmetry, generalized metric, and non-Riemannian sectors.

Field Equation

GAB = TAB as a proposed field equation for closed-string gravity.

Matter

O(D,D)-covariant coupling to particles, strings, spinors, gauge fields, and the Standard Model.

Phenomenology

Post-Newtonian gravity, cosmology possibly without a dark sector, black holes, wormholes, and gravitational waves.
