Gravity of Closed Strings

My research centers on one question:

What is the gravitational theory naturally implied by closed string theory?

Research is driven by questions, not by calculations.1

This program asks whether Double Field Theory provides the natural geometric completion of the universal closed-string massless sector {g, B, φ}, linking string theory, gravity, geometry, cosmology, and phenomenology.

Research Vision

The question is not whether string theory contains gravity. It does. The question is how string theory organizes gravity. Closed strings contain the universal massless sector {g, B, φ}. General Relativity successfully geometrizes the metric. The remaining question is how to geometrize the complete closed-string massless sector.

Working Hypothesis This program asks whether Double Field Theory provides the natural geometric framework for the universal closed-string massless sector.

Research Themes

Geometry

Doubled-yet-gauged spacetime, coordinate gauge symmetry, generalized metric, and non-Riemannian sectors.

Dynamics

GAB = TAB as a proposed field equation for closed-string gravity.

Matter

O(D,D)-covariant coupling to particles, strings, spinors, gauge fields, and the Standard Model.

Phenomenology

Post-Newtonian gravity, cosmology without a dark sector, black holes, wormholes, and gravitational waves.
